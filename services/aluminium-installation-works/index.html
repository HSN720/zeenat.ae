The line

A metre of extrusionis easy. Sixty is not.

Any fabricator can cut one profile square. The discipline is holding that same line across an entire elevation — through thermal movement, structural deflection and a tolerance stack that grows with every floor.

We set out against a surveyed grid rather than the architect’s nominal dimensions, and we specify thermal breaks for a Gulf summer rather than a European one. Joints stay even because they were planned to, not because they were forced.

Scope

What we install

  • 01

    Curtain wall systems

    Stick and unitised curtain walling set out to a survey-verified grid, with thermal breaks specified for Gulf summer loads.

  • 02

    Composite cladding

    Aluminium composite and solid panel rainscreens, with concealed fixings and joint widths held consistent across the elevation.

  • 03

    Windows & door sets

    Sliding, casement, tilt-and-turn and pivot sets, fabricated to opening sizes taken on site rather than off the drawing.

  • 04

    Louvres & screening

    Solar shading, plant screening and privacy louvres, aligned to the facade module so nothing reads as an afterthought.

Powder coator anodised.

The finish is not a colour decision — it is a durability one. Coastal air, direct sun and cleaning regimes all pull at a facade differently, and the right answer changes with orientation.

We will tell you which one the elevation needs, including when the cheaper option is the correct one.
